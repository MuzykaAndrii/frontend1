About project:

The service intended to simplify learning of english vocabulary. Works as telegram bot, have web representation for administrative purpose (admin dashboard). After creating vocabulary you will receive notifications during the day (every 20 mins from 10pm to 22am) with random word from active vocabulary. The notification will include the sentence example generated by ChatGPT. The philosophy is to memorize lexic frequently seeing them (also known as Anki method).

Technical stack:
  • FastAPI
  • Starlette-admin
  • Aiogram
  • SQLAlchemy
  • Alembic
